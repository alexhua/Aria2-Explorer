Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Aria2 Explorer captures download tasks and related website cookies from your browser solely to connect to your Aria2 server for downloading network resources.

All Aria2 connection and configuration information is stored locally on your device. You may optionally upload settings to your logged-in cloud account for synchronization across devices.
